About Me

As a dedicated student pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics and Project Management at the University of Connecticut, my academic and professional journey is built on a diverse foundation—beginning with a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering and further honed through rigorous coursework in Data Science, Predictive Analytics, Statistics, Quantitative Analysis, Data Engineering, and Big Data Management.

My professional experience includes a Systems Engineer role at Tata Consultancy Services, where I developed and implemented a CI/CD test utility using Jenkins that reduced infrastructure costs by $100K, automated report generation to save 85% of reporting time, and led multiple proof-of-concept projects to enhance data privacy and quality through SQL-driven data cleaning initiatives.

With solid achievements and certifications in SQL, Python, JIRA, and Agile methodologies—alongside proficiency in Tableau, Power BI, Azure Data Factory, and Databricks—I am well-prepared to tackle complex challenges at the intersection of business analytics and project management.

I am also actively engaged in academic projects that apply analytical techniques to real-world problems, such as healthcare claims database optimization, risk prediction in car insurance claims, and nutritional analysis of consumer products. When I’m not working on data models, I enjoy playing outdoor sports, watching movies, and exploring emerging technologies.
